Year 3

Strengthened Disaster Risk Reduction Measures

Our initiative has three primary goals:

Empower Communities: We aim to cultivate a preparedness culture and reduce vulnerability by providing communities with the tools and knowledge they need to face disasters confidently.

Reduce Disaster Impact: Through real-time information and support, we strive to save lives and minimize the impact of disasters on communities.

Enhance Community Resilience: We seek to strengthen communities with the necessary resources and knowledge for self-reliance during disasters, enabling them to bounce back more effectively.

Method

Proactive measures, including training and communication, can lead to a 30-40% reduction in disaster-related fatalities and injuries.

-National Center for Disaster Preparedness at Columbia University



Emergency Response Plans: Users can create personalized household plans that consider specific hazards, available resources, and household composition. First Aid Training: We provide instructional videos and resources for basic first aid and CPR, empowering individuals to respond effectively to medical emergencies. Evacuation Routes: Reliable evacuation routes and safe locations are provided to facilitate quick and safe movement during disasters. Community Communication: We facilitate vital information sharing among community members, fostering mutual support during emergencies.
